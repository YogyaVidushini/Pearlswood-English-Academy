About Us

Welcome to Pearlswood English Academy

At Pearlswood, we believe English is the key to endless opportunities. Our expert tutors help students master grammar, reading, writing, speaking, and listening with confidence. We offer specialized training for Cambridge English exams and provide focused preparation for O/L and A/L examinations. Whether you are building your foundation or aiming for top grades, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, engaging, and results-driven English education that equips students with the skills and confidence to excel in exams and everyday communication.

Our Vision

To be the leading English language academy that empowers students to communicate confidently and achieve global success.

Acheivements

Over the years, Pearlswood English Academy has celebrated countless student successes. Our learners have excelled in Cambridge English exams and achieved top scores in O/L and A/L English. Beyond academics, they have shined in language competitions, interactive workshops, and extracurricular activities, steadily building confidence in speaking, writing, and listening. These achievements reflect the dedication of our students and the quality guidance of our experienced faculty, inspiring us to continue fostering excellence in every learner.

Our Programs



  • Starters, Movers, Flyers (YLE): Fun and interactive English learning for young learners.


  • KET,PET, FCE: Build strong English reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills for international exams.

  • Classes tailored for students from Grade 1 to 13.


  • Focused preparation for O/L and A/L examinations.

  • Grammar & Writing: Clear, structured, and engaging lessons.


  • Speaking & Listening: Regular practice through conversations, role-plays, and presentations.


  • Monthly Evaluations: Tests and assignments to track progress and reinforce learning.

  • Exam-focused workshops to strengthen weak areas.


  • Interactive group sessions to boost confidence and communication skills.
